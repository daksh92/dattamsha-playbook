The 4-Step Strategy: Bridging Every Divide

We address the fundamental challenges facing modern HR functions with a structured, phased approach to adoption and value delivery.

STEP 1

The Data OS

Problem: The Data Divide. Siloed HR, ATS, and Payroll data.

Solution: Unified Data Fabric. A productized data engineering layer with guaranteed-SLA connectors to establish a single source of truth in weeks.

Outcome: 70% reduction in custom data engineering costs.

STEP 2

The Financial Brain

Problem: The Insights Divide. Descriptive, non-financially linked reports.

Solution: P&L-Linked Insight Modules. Predictive analytics (e.g., Attrition Cost Modeler) that integrate with finance systems to show real ROI.

Outcome: Justify HR initiatives in the language of the CFO.

STEP 3

The Agentic Co-pilot

Problem: The Action Divide. Insights require manual analyst queries and execution.

Solution: Agentic AI Layer. A premium suite of AI agents (Analyst, Compliance, Workflow) that execute multi-step tasks across systems autonomously.

Outcome: Move from reporting to automated action execution.

STEP 4

The Nudge Engine

Problem: The Behavior Gap. Insights fail to drive human behavioral change.

Solution: Behavioral Nudge Engine. Contextual, science-backed micro-interventions delivered at the optimal time to drive measurable habit change and adoption.

Outcome: Instant "Aha!" moment for PLG; sustained positive colleague experiences.

The Agentic Layer: Automated Action Execution

The Agentic Co-pilot eliminates the manual effort involved in complex HR processes, turning sophisticated insights into cross-system execution.

The Analyst Agent

Enables natural language querying of the entire data fabric. Example query: “Show me the correlation between manager effectiveness scores and engineering attrition in the APAC region over the last 18 months.”

The Workflow Agent

Automates complex, multi-step processes. Example: Automates onboarding—provisioning IT, generating compliance documents, and scheduling 30-60-90 day manager check-ins.

The Compliance Agent

Proactively scans employee data against global labor laws and visa deadlines to flag and prevent compliance risks before they result in legal issues.

The Nudge Engine: Productizing Behavioral Science

Behavioral Nudges are the final, critical layer, ensuring that organizational change is driven by contextual, science-backed micro-interventions, not passive reporting.

Contextual Micro-Interventions

The engine moves beyond generic, mass communication. It uses AI to determine the optimal timing, context, and channel for delivering relevant content based on the employee's role, location, and activity data.

  • AI-Led Employee Listening: Utilizes contextual intelligence to deploy "One Question a Day" micro-surveys for a real-time sentiment pulse.
  • Science-Backed Framework: All nudges are based on rigorous behavioral science principles to sustain or improve priority drivers of positive colleague experiences.

Strategic Value & PLG Entry Point

The Nudge Engine is strategically positioned as the viral entry point for our Product-Led Growth model, offering immediate, tangible value that managers and HRBPs desperately need.

  • Instant "Aha!" Moment: Low-friction product that shows measurable behavioral shifts within weeks (e.g., increased performance review completion rates).
  • Low-Risk Adoption: Ideal for departmental subscription trials, demonstrating value before the enterprise-wide upsell of the Data Fabric and Agentic Layer.
